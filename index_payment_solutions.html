Who Can Open an Account

Anyone with legal residency in UK

For long-term residents and immigrants living in the UK, BilinkPay provides secure and transparent everyday financial services with bilingual support for effortless money management.

Designed for international students and young professionals, BilinkPay makes it simple to open an account using your passport or student visa — helping you manage tuition, living costs, and daily payments.

For travellers and short-term visitors, BilinkPay offers convenient payment and foreign exchange services through multi-currency wallets and virtual cards, ensuring flexibility during your stay.

For business owners and SMEs, BilinkPay supports multi-currency business accounts and settlements, helping you manage operations and cross-border transactions securely within the UK and EU.
